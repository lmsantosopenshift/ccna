Wireless LAN Configuration

Configuring Wireless Clients

When the AP or wireless router has been configured, the wireless NIC on the client must be configured to allow it to connect to the WLAN. The user should also verify that the client has successfully connected to the correct wireless network, especially because there may be many WLANs available with which to connect.
